Custom operators (macros)

One-argument templates over x, e.g. exp(-square(x)), expanded into the expression as the search builds it. Complexity counts the expanded nodes, results print the primitive form, and numbers in a body become tunable constants seeded at that value. A body may use operators that are not checked above. Off by default — with no macros the search is unchanged.
